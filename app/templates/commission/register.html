Commission Partner Terms and Conditions
1. Commission Structure

Partners earn 20% commission on initial sign-ups and 2.5% recurring commission on monthly payments. Commissions are calculated based on the service type selected by the client.

2. Payment Terms

Commissions are paid monthly, on the 15th of each month, for all commissions marked as "paid" in the previous month. A minimum payout threshold of $50 applies.

3. Referral Network

Partners can refer other partners to the program. When your referred partners generate commissions, you do not receive a percentage of their earnings.

4. Commission Eligibility

Commissions are only earned when a referred client signs up for a paid plan. Commissions are not earned for free trials or demo bookings.

5. Termination

We reserve the right to terminate any partner account for violations of these terms, including but not limited to fraudulent activity, misrepresentation, or spam.

6. Changes to Terms

We may modify these terms at any time. Partners will be notified of any changes via email.
