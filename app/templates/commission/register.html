Commission Partner Terms and Conditions
1. Commission Structure

Direct Client Referrals:

  • 10% commission on all client payments for the first two years
  • 2.5% commission on all client payments from year three onwards

Network Partner Earnings:

  • 2.5% commission on all monthly payments from your network partners' clients
  • Network commissions are not time-limited and continue as long as the clients remain active
2. Payment Terms

Commissions are paid monthly, on the 15th of each month, for all commissions marked as "paid" in the previous month. A minimum payout threshold of $50 applies.

3. Commission Eligibility

To be eligible for any commissions (both direct referrals and network earnings):

  • You must be a registered partner and have accepted these terms and conditions
  • Commissions are only earned when referred clients sign up for a paid plan
  • Commissions are not earned for free trials or demo bookings
  • The client must maintain an active, paid subscription
4. Network Building

As a registered partner, you can:

  • Share your unique referral code to build your network
  • Earn network commissions from all clients brought in by your network partners
  • Access tools and resources to help grow your network
5. Termination

We reserve the right to terminate any partner account for violations of these terms, including but not limited to:

  • Fraudulent activity or misrepresentation
  • Spam or aggressive marketing tactics
  • Violation of our terms of service
6. Changes to Terms

We may modify these terms at any time. Partners will be notified of any changes via email. Continued participation in the program after changes constitutes acceptance of the new terms.
