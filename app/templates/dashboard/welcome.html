Getting Started

What is the LOS Referral Program?

Welcome to the Logistics One Source Referral Program! This platform allows you to refer potential clients to our services and earn rewards for successful referrals. As your referrals progress through our sales pipeline, you'll earn points that can be redeemed for various rewards.

How It Works

1. Share Your Link

Share your unique referral link with potential clients or partners.

2. Track Progress

Monitor your referrals as they move through our sales process.

3. Earn Rewards

Earn points and commissions as your referrals convert to clients.

Points & Rewards System

Our points system rewards you at every stage of the referral process:

  • Lead Generation: 2 points
  • Engagement: 5 points
  • Completion: 15 points
  • Conversion: 50 points
  • Retention: 25 points
  • Upsell: 35 points
  • Partner Network: 25 points
