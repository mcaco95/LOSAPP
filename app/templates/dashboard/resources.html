Recruitment Commission Structure

Partner Recruitment

10%

Unlike the safety commission:

This recruitment commission is a one-time 10% payment for each successful referral your recruits bring in. It's a per-basis commission rather than the recurring safety commission structure.

How It Works

1. Recruit Partners

Share your network code with potential partners who could refer clients to LOS.

2. They Refer Clients

When your recruited partners successfully refer clients who sign up for LOS services.

Frequently Asked Questions

Getting started is simple! Just log in to your account, go to your profile page to get your unique referral link, and start sharing it with potential clients. You'll earn points and commissions for every successful referral.

Commissions are paid monthly on the 15th for all commissions marked as "paid" in the previous month. Payments are made directly to your registered payment method.

If a client cancels their service, you'll continue to receive commissions for any payments made up until the cancellation date. Future commissions will cease after the cancellation is processed.

You can track all your referrals, points, and commissions through your dashboard. It provides real-time updates on the status of your referrals and detailed earnings reports.

Yes, you can refer clients from any country where our services are available. Contact support for specific details about service availability in different regions.

As a commission partner, you can invite others to join our partner program. When someone joins through your network code and brings in clients:

  • You earn 2.5% of their clients' monthly payments
  • This is a direct, single-level program (not a pyramid scheme)
  • Your network commission is paid monthly alongside your direct commissions
  • There's no limit to how many partners you can bring to the program
