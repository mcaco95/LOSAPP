Program Overview

What is the LOS Referral Program?

The LOS Referral Program allows you to earn rewards by referring businesses that need logistics services to Logistics One Source. It's simple:

  • Share your unique referral link with potential clients
  • When they fill out the form, our sales team takes over
  • You earn points and commissions for successful referrals
  • Track everything through your personalized dashboard

Our internal sales team handles all client interactions after the initial referral, so you don't need to worry about follow-ups or closing deals.

How It Works

1. Share Your Link

Share your unique referral link with potential clients who need logistics services

2. Client Submits Form

When they click your link and submit the form, our system tracks it as your referral

3. Sales Team Takes Over

Our internal sales team contacts the lead and handles the entire sales process

4. You Earn Rewards

When the referral becomes a client, you earn points and commission

System Features:

  • Real-time tracking of all your referrals
  • Personalized dashboard to monitor progress
  • Automatic point accumulation
  • Commission tracking and history
  • Reward redemption options
  • Performance analytics
